# Why this project is timely

ThreadPass PLM sits at the intersection of several active PLM themes:

1. **Digital Product Passports (DPPs)** are becoming strategically important because the EU's Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR) introduced DPPs and the first ESPR / Energy Labelling Working Plan was adopted in April 2025.
2. **Engineering change management and digital thread** work increasingly emphasizes connected lifecycle data rather than isolated document silos.
3. **Hybrid BOMs** are becoming more relevant as physical products carry embedded software and SBOM-style traceability requirements.

Useful references you can cite in discussions or presentations:

- European Commission: Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation and Digital Product Passport
- Odoo PLM documentation: Engineering change orders and BOM revision workflows
- openPLM: open-source PLM with BOM, revisions, and traceability
- CycloneDX specification: support for SBOM, HBOM, and MBOM
- Design Society / Cambridge Core (2024): Digital thread with a native graph database in PLM
