# Project Overview

## One-line pitch

ThreadPass PLM is a composable PLM microservice that turns BOM and ECO data into digital product passports and graph-style change-impact reports.

## Problem statement

Many PLM examples on GitHub either try to clone a full enterprise PLM suite or stop at CRUD around parts and documents. That makes them heavy, generic, or difficult to demo.

A stronger open-source portfolio project is narrower and easier to understand:

- ingest a product structure
- identify which products are hit by a component change
- surface lifecycle and compliance implications
- generate a portable passport-like artifact for transparency and service

## Target users

- PLM architects
- manufacturing software engineers
- digital thread teams
- sustainability and compliance teams
- service engineering teams
- portfolio reviewers evaluating your system design skills

## What makes it interesting

- It demonstrates **domain awareness** beyond CRUD.
- It shows **graph reasoning** without requiring a heavy graph database.
- It connects **engineering change**, **sustainability**, **software traceability**, and **serviceability**.
- It is small enough to run locally in minutes.
